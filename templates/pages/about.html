About

Hi! my name is Luis Portillo, Venezuelan Fullstack Web Developer (DevCorvus). I have recently tried Django so following the line of frameworks for Python, now I wanted to try Flask. In terms of development, I didn't want to delve too deeply into Flask since it is not a tool that could predictably be on my priority list compared to other options, so it was quite fast but a pleasant experience since it is similar to what Express is for NodeJs.

Concluding this small project, I will move to other plans that I have in mind.
